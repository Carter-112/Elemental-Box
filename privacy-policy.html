Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of all visitors to ElementalBox. This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and use.

Data Collection

ElementalBox does not collect any personal data from its users. We do not track your activity, store cookies for tracking purposes, or collect any personal information.

How the Application Works

ElementalBox is a particle sandbox that runs entirely in your browser. Any data created during your session (such as saved simulations) is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage feature and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

ElementalBox may display advertisements through third-party ad providers. These providers may use cookies or similar technologies for ad targeting and measurement. This is standard practice for ad-supported applications and websites. The information collected by these third parties is subject to their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us using our contact form.
